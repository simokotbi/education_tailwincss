Admission Requirements

Undergraduate Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA of 3.5 (on 4.0 scale)
  • SAT (1350+) or ACT (30+) scores
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Personal statement essay
  • Official transcripts
  • English proficiency test (for international students)
  • Portfolio or additional materials (program-specific)

Graduate Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree from accredited institution
  • Minimum undergraduate GPA of 3.3
  • GRE/GMAT scores (program-dependent)
  • Three letters of recommendation
  • Statement of purpose
  • Professional resume/CV
  • Official transcripts from all institutions
  • Work experience (for certain programs)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the application fee?

The application fee is $75 for domestic students and $100 for international students. Fee waivers are available for qualifying applicants.

Do you accept transfer students?

Yes, we welcome transfer students. You can transfer up to 90 credit hours toward your degree, depending on the program and course equivalency.

What English proficiency scores do you accept?

We accept TOEFL (minimum 100), IELTS (minimum 7.0), or Duolingo English Test (minimum 120) for international students whose first language is not English.

Can I defer my admission?

Yes, admitted students may request to defer their enrollment for up to one year. A written request must be submitted by the enrollment deadline.
