Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a campus visit?

You can schedule a campus visit through our online booking system or by calling our admissions office at +1 (617) 555-0200. We offer both individual tours and group information sessions.

What's the best way to contact specific departments?

Each academic department has dedicated contact information available on our website. For general inquiries, start with our main number, and we'll connect you with the appropriate department.

Do you offer virtual information sessions?

Yes! We offer virtual information sessions, webinars, and online tours for prospective students who cannot visit campus. Check our events calendar for upcoming virtual sessions.

How can international students get support?

Our International Student Services office provides comprehensive support including visa assistance, cultural orientation, and academic guidance. Contact them at international@nexusacademy.edu.
